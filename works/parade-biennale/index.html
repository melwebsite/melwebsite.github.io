  • Performers appear caught in a process of game-like ritualistic actions. They perform, struggle, play, resist with the objects and the environment they are in. As they react and relate, they themselves become part of the site. Specifying the group from everyday life, the stage like environment is liminal in nature. You could say they are in limbo.

    During the Biennale of Sydney sixty eight performers kindly volunteered their time to perform four times a day, seven days a week for a total of 324 performances.
