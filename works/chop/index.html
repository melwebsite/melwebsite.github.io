  • 'Chop'is set in a cavernous and confined interior space. We watch as a man undertakes the physically demanding task of breaking apart a large rock with his hammer. His struggle and fatigue are palpable. This film is to be projected by 16mm projector at a small frame size to further emphasis the claustrophobic and intense nature of the space and sound.
