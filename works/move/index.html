  • 'Move' is set in an overgrown ruin and framed by fallen stones. A group of four performers move rocks from one pile to another. As each rock is chopped in half, the amount of rocks multiplies. With each new batch of rocks brought by wheelbarrow the amount rocks exponentially increases.
