  • In 'Endgame' we watch as a group of performers inhabit a open air ruin and undertake a series of repetitive like actions. These games are set to the sound of a pendulum that inversely moves to the beat of the actions themselves. The programmed shuffle further emphasises the endless nature of the repetitive motions.
